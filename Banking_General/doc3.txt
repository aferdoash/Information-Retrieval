KINDS OF CARDS ACCEPTED AT STATE BANK ATMs
Besides all cards of State Bank of India, State Bank ATM-Cum-Debit Card and State Bank International ATM-Cum-Debit Cards following cards are also accepted at State Bank ATMs:
State Bank Credit Card
Cards issued by other banks displaying Maestro, Master Card, Cirrus, VISA and VISA Electron logos
All Debit/ Credit Cards issued by any bank outside India displaying Maestro, Master Card, Cirrus, VISA and VISA Electron logos
